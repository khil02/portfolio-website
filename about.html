About Me

My story

After moving around the country as a child, we settled down in North Carolina, where I went to college at the University of North Carolina Charlotte. While I took my programming and video game design classes, I ended up getting my degreee in Business Administration. While in college I worked for a non-profit as their Database Administrator; after college I went into the mortgage industry and became a Mortgage Underwriter. At the start of 2022 my wife changed jobs and became a travelling nurse to let us follow one of our shared passions, travel! Just as that adventure was starting the housing market took a downturn and I realized that wanted out of that industry and into something I enjoyed more and would have the flexibility to fit into our crazy travelling lives; so I went back to programming albeit with a broader focus than I had in college.
