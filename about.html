About Me

My story

After moving around the country as a child, my family settled down in North Carolina where I went to college at the University of North Carolina Charlotte. While I took my programming and video game design classes, I ended up getting my degreee in Business Administration. While in college I worked for a non-profit as their Database Administrator; after college I went into the mortgage industry and became a Mortgage Underwriter. At the start of 2022, my wife changed jobs and became a travelling nurse to let us follow one of our shared passions, travel! Just as that adventure was starting the housing market took a downturn and I realized that I wanted out of that industry and into something I enjoyed more and allowed more flexibility. With that goal in mind, I remembered how much I enjoyed programming in college and decided to make that my new focus.

Based on my past experience solving problems, I am ready to apply the same tenacity to problem solving- with code.
